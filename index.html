Plant Breeding

Plant breeding is a science-driven process aimed at developing new and improved plant varieties with desirable traits such as higher yield, disease resistance, drought tolerance, and nutritional quality. It involves careful selection and crossbreeding of plants to create offspring with specific genetic characteristics that are beneficial for agriculture and food production.

Plantation establishment and Management

Plantation establishment and management involve the process of establishing and maintaining large-scale plantations of trees, crops, or other agricultural commodities. This is a complex and multifaceted endeavor that requires a combination of scientific knowledge, technical expertise, and effective management strategies to achieve optimal productivity, profitability, and environmental stewardship.

Read More

Agro-Processing and Training

Agro-processing and training play crucial roles in enhancing agricultural value chains, promoting economic development, and empowering communities. Agro-processing involves the conversion of raw agricultural products into value-added products such as food, beverages, textiles, and biofuels which adds value to agricultural commodities, reduces post-harvest losses, and creates opportunities for income generation and job creation along the supply chain.
